# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

The NotchNudge team and community take security issues seriously. We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose any vulnerabilities you find, and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

To report a security issue, please use the GitHub Security Advisory ["Report a Vulnerability"](https://github.com/yourusername/notchnudge/security/advisories/new) tab.

The NotchNudge team will respond with next steps for handling your report. After the initial reply, we will keep you informed of progress toward a fix and announcement, and may request additional information or guidance.

Please report security bugs in third-party dependencies to the maintainers of those packages or dependencies.
